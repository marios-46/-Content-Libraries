Anime libraries are uniquely complex. Unlike general video platforms, anime content includes seasons, arcs, spin-offs, movies, and alternative releases. Titles may exist in multiple formats or languages, further complicating organization.

Without intelligent systems, platforms risk cluttered interfaces, slow discovery, and inconsistent categorization as libraries grow.
